Figure R1 — Examples of model collapse and fragmented prediction on BNCI 2014

Figure R1. Examples of model collapse and fragmented prediction on BNCI 2014. Qualitative comparison of decoded event timelines on the BNCI2014 motor imagery dataset (4-class: Tongue, Feet, Left Hand, Right Hand) showing a 70 s excerpt from a held-out test session. Each row displays the ground-truth (GT) events and predictions from DANCE, CBraMod, U-Net, ATCNet, and EEGSym. Solid borders denote correct detections (prediction overlaps a same-class GT event by more than 50%), while dashed borders indicate false positives. Event-level F1 (F1e) and sample-level F1 (F1s) are reported on the right margin, computed over the full timeline.
