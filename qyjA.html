Figure R3 — Decoupled metric for localization and temporal precision

Figure R3. DANCE performs better than the best baseline on both metrics when the arrow points to the upper left. This plot shows a joint view of detection quality (F1detect, y-axis) and temporal precision (center prediction error in seconds, x-axis, log scale), comparing DANCE (colored circles) to the best non-ablation baseline (gray diamonds). F1det is a class-agnostic detection at IoU > 0.5 to quantify retrieval independent of classification. Δctr is the median center-point error in seconds, computed on matched events, to directly quantify temporal precision in physical units. Δctr is conditional on matching, and therefore must be interpreted jointly with F1 metrics.
